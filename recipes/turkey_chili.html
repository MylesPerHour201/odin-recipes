Spicy Turkey Chili

Description

This a very hearty and spicy chili, one I believe all will love! Serve topped with Cheddar cheese.

Ingredients

    2 cans of turkey meat, drained 2 cans of kidney beans 2 cans of Italian-style stewed tomatoes 2 packages of chili seasoning mix 1 can of green chile peppers 1 can of tomato sauce 1 onion, diced 1 cup of water

Steps

    In a slow cooker, combine all ingredients. Cook on low for 3 to 4 hours. Serve hot.
